Key Trends & Insights

This analysis reveals significant patterns in religious sentiment expression on Facebook in India:

Rapid Growth Phase (2010-2013): Both user base and religious engagement saw exponential growth as Facebook gained popularity in India.

Volatility Period (2014-2017): Religious posts became increasingly politicized, correlating with real-world events. The 2017 Ayodhya verdict caused the highest engagement spike.

Peak and Regulation (2020-2025): COVID-19 lockdowns led to a surge in virtual religious activities (2020). Increased regulation in 2022-2025 moderated engagement despite growing user base.

Note the divergence between user growth and engagement patterns after 2020, suggesting platform saturation and regulatory impacts.
